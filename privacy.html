Privacy Policy

Application: Suno-auto

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Suno-auto respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary: your TikTok access token (to post videos on your behalf) and basic account information provided by the TikTok API.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information is used solely to upload AI-generated music videos to your TikTok account. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to any third parties.

4. Data Storage

Suno-auto runs locally on your machine. No personal data is stored on external servers. Access tokens are stored only on your local device.

5. TikTok Permissions

When you connect your TikTok account, we access only the permissions you explicitly grant. You can revoke access at any time via TikTok account settings under "Apps and Websites".

6. Security

Your credentials are not transmitted to any third-party servers other than TikTok's official API endpoints.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
