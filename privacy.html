Suno-auto Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026


  1. Introduction
  2. Suno-auto respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary: your TikTok access token (to post videos on your behalf) and basic account info provided by TikTok API.


3. How We Use Your Information

Information is used solely to upload AI-generated music videos to your TikTok account. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to third parties.


4. Data Storage

Suno-auto runs locally on your machine. No personal data is stored on external servers. Access tokens are stored only on your local device.


5. TikTok Permissions

When you connect your TikTok account, we access only permissions you explicitly grant. You can revoke access anytime via TikTok account settings under Apps and Websites.


6. Security

Your credentials are not transmitted to any third-party servers other than TikTok official API endpoints.
